WELCOME TO BALLINTICA

A THEATRICAL BALLET DANCE TROUPE

This is Ballintica. We're a local based Melbourne dance troupe specialising in classical theatrical ballet dancing. We had been peforming in numerous theaters and dance festivals throughout Melbourne. Our aim and goal is to spread the beauty of theatrical ballet and insire many about the artistical manner of this long line history of ballet peformance.

Dancer Biography

Get to know more about our Ballarina

Jessy Linster

Born in Ballarat, Jessy has a living passion of peforming ballet dancing from a very young age of 5. She has been regconised as one of the best dancers among Ballarat and now would want to share the same passions with other ballerinas.

Olivia Drow

From the young age of 12, Olivia has been a lead dancer at the Frankston High School Ballet Dance Club, and has numerous 1st place at prestigious ballet competition under her name. She's now part of Ballintica as she believe that this style of ballet has the potential to bring a whole new life to Ballet dancing.

Charlotte Lee

Charlotte originally starting her passion in ballet dancing from her previous home country of Malaysia. She has beeen recognised by her very peers back at her home country to be one of the best dancers within the country and now is bringing that talent to Melbourne to showcase what she is capable of.

Sophia Hernande

"Ballet is one that to do to understand the completixty of it". Raised in the town of Geelong, Sophia Hernanade has been dancing ballet every since. She has travelled accross the world to learn and study the diverse types of ballet dancing from every culture alive. She has now part of Ballintica to spread the style of ballet she has learnt over her experience.

Emma Smith

Emma has only one thing she would ever do, it is peforming ballet in front of a crowd. Born in North Melbourne, her parents believes she could be the best ballerina that Victoria could ever have and has been supportive in her passion. She has won several ballerina awards and now part of the Ballintica dance troupe to showcase her ballet dances over a large audience to spectate the beauty of it.
